**Document Type:** Feature Overview

## Overview

Prakrit.ai slots into Apollo 247 as the patient engagement layer — the part that keeps people connected to their health between appointments, purchases, and tests. Right now there's a gap: Apollo handles the healthcare transactions brilliantly, but once a patient leaves the app, they're on their own. Prakrit fills that gap with continuous, AI-powered health management, and feeds patients right back into Apollo's ecosystem when they need care.

## Integration by Apollo Product Area

### Consultations
- The AI Health Assistant kicks in right after a consultation ends, so the relationship doesn't go cold
- PrakritGPT handles between-visit questions and routes clinical ones toward a new consultation
- Prescription data flows automatically into medication tracking the moment a consultation closes

### Diagnostics
- Lab reports are explained in plain language as soon as they're delivered
- The Health Timeline pulls all test history together so patients can see how they're trending over time
- The Health Insights engine runs comparative analysis across reports — spotting patterns a patient would never catch on their own

### Pharmacy
- The Medication Tracker calculates refill dates and sends deep-link reminders straight to Apollo Pharmacy
- Adherence monitoring catches drop-off before it turns into missed refills

### Apollo Circle
- Family Health Profiles replace the discount-focused Circle dashboard with something genuinely useful
- Every family member gets individual health tracking, medication management, and document storage

### Speciality Programs
- ProHealth and Diabetes Reversal patients get Prakrit as their daily AI companion between instructor sessions
- MomVerse gains active pregnancy tracking, document analysis, and a newborn profile after delivery

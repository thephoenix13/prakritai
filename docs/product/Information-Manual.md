## 1. Getting Started

### Creating your account
1. Open Prakrit AI and tap **Sign Up**
2. Enter your name, email address, and a password (minimum 6 characters)
3. You'll be taken directly to your dashboard — no email confirmation needed

### Logging in
- Enter your email and password, then tap **Login**
- If you've forgotten your password, tap **Forgot Password** and a reset link will be sent to your email

### Your dashboard
The dashboard is your home base. From here you can see a summary of your family's health activity, access all features, and pick up where you left off. The bottom navigation bar gives you quick access to the most-used sections: Home, Family, AI Assistant, Medications, Documents, and SOS.

## 2. Adding Family Members

Prakrit AI is built around family. Each person you add gets their own health profile — separate records, documents, medications, and timeline.

### Adding a family member
1. Go to the **Family** tab from the bottom navigation
2. Tap **Add Family Member**
3. Enter their name, relationship (e.g. spouse, parent, child), date of birth, and blood type if known
4. Tap **Save**

### Viewing a family member's profile
Tap any family member's card to open their profile. From there you can view their documents, medications, health timeline, and AI analysis results.

### Editing or removing a family member
Open the family member's profile and tap the edit icon in the top right. You can update their details or remove them from your account.

> **Tip:** Add all family members upfront — even with minimal details. You can always fill in more information later, and having them set up means documents and medications can be assigned to the right person from the start.

## 3. Uploading & Analysing Documents

This is one of Prakrit AI's most useful features. You can upload any medical document and get a plain-language explanation of what it contains.

### Supported document types
- Lab reports and blood tests
- Doctor prescriptions
- Scan reports (X-ray, MRI, ultrasound)
- Discharge summaries
- Any other medical PDF or image

### Uploading a document
1. Go to the **Documents** tab or open a family member's profile
2. Tap **Upload Document**
3. Choose the family member the document belongs to
4. Select the document type
5. Upload from your phone's gallery, take a photo with your camera, or upload a PDF
6. Add any notes if helpful (optional)
7. Tap **Save**

### Getting an AI analysis
After uploading, tap **Analyse with AI** on any document. Prakrit AI will:
- Read the document and identify key values, medications, or findings
- Explain what each value means in plain language
- Flag anything that's outside the normal range
- Suggest what to discuss at your next doctor's visit

The analysis is saved to the document, so you can refer back to it anytime.

### Viewing past documents
All uploaded documents appear in the Documents section, organised by family member and date. Tap any document to view it along with its AI analysis.

## 4. Medication Tracking

Prakrit AI makes it straightforward to track medications for every family member and stay on top of schedules and refills.

### Adding a medication
1. Go to the **Medications** tab
2. Tap **Add Medication**
3. Select the family member
4. Enter the medication name, dosage, frequency (e.g. twice daily), and start date
5. Add the quantity if you want refill tracking
6. Tap **Save**

### Medication reminders
Once a medication is added, Prakrit AI tracks the schedule. You'll receive push notifications at the times the medication is due. If a dose is missed, you'll get a follow-up reminder.

### Refill alerts
If you enter the quantity of a medication, Prakrit AI calculates when it will run out based on the dosage and frequency, and sends you an alert before it does.

### Marking doses as taken
Open the Medications tab to see today's schedule. Tap the checkmark next to a medication to mark it as taken. You can also view the full history of doses taken for any medication.

### Editing or stopping a medication
Tap any medication to open its details. You can edit the dosage, frequency, or other details, or mark it as stopped if the course has ended.

> **Tip:** If a prescription is uploaded as a document, Prakrit AI can read the medications from it. You can use the analysis to quickly add them to your tracker without typing everything manually.

## 5. AI Health Assistant

The AI Health Assistant is available 24/7 to answer health questions, help you understand medical information, and keep track of your family's wellbeing.

### Starting a conversation
1. Tap **AI Assistant** in the bottom navigation
2. Type your question or tap the microphone icon to speak
3. The assistant responds immediately

### What you can ask
- Follow-up questions after a doctor's visit: *"My doctor mentioned elevated creatinine — what does that mean?"*
- Help understanding documents: *"Can you explain the results from my CBC report?"*
- Medication questions: *"Is it okay to take ibuprofen with the blood pressure medication I'm on?"*
- General health guidance: *"What's a healthy HbA1c range for someone with Type 2 diabetes?"*
- Symptom check-ins: *"I've had a persistent headache for 3 days — should I be worried?"*

The assistant has context from your family's health history in Prakrit AI, so it can give more relevant answers than a generic search engine.

### Voice mode
Tap the microphone icon to switch to voice mode. Speak your question naturally — the assistant will respond in audio. This is particularly useful when you're on the go or prefer talking to typing.

### Conversation history
Your conversations are saved. You can scroll back through past exchanges and continue any previous conversation. The assistant remembers context within a conversation, so you don't need to repeat yourself.

> **Important:** The AI Health Assistant provides information and guidance — it is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a qualified doctor for diagnosis or treatment decisions.

## 6. Health Insights

Health Insights lets you ask Prakrit AI to look across multiple documents and find patterns or trends over time.

### Running a health insight
1. Go to **Health Insights** from the dashboard or menu
2. Select the family member
3. Choose the documents you want included in the analysis (or select all)
4. Tap **Generate Insights**

### What you get
Prakrit AI reviews all selected documents together and produces a summary covering:
- Key trends across test results (e.g. improving or worsening values over time)
- Notable changes since the last set of results
- Areas to watch or discuss with your doctor
- A timeline of significant findings

This is especially useful before a doctor's appointment — you'll walk in knowing exactly what's changed since the last visit.

## 7. Health Timeline

The Health Timeline gives you a chronological view of a family member's entire health history inside Prakrit AI.

### Viewing the timeline
Open any family member's profile and tap **Timeline**. You'll see all their documents, medications started or stopped, AI analysis results, and any notes — sorted by date.

### What appears on the timeline
- Documents uploaded and their AI analysis summaries
- Medications added or discontinued
- Health Insights generated
- Any notes you've added

The timeline becomes more valuable over time. As more records build up, patterns become clearer and the history becomes a genuinely useful reference for both you and your doctor.

## 8. Emergency Info & SOS Card

The Emergency Info section stores critical health data that could be needed in an emergency — quickly accessible, even from the lock screen shortcut.

### Setting up your emergency info
1. Tap **SOS** in the bottom navigation
2. Tap **Edit Emergency Info**
3. Fill in:
   - Blood type
   - Known allergies
   - Current medications (can be pulled from your medication tracker)
   - Emergency contacts (name and phone number)
   - Any critical medical conditions
4. Tap **Save**

### Sharing your emergency card
Your emergency info can be shared as a card or QR code. In an emergency, anyone with the card — a doctor, paramedic, or family member — can instantly see your critical health information.

### SOS button
The SOS button in the navigation bar gives immediate access to your emergency contacts. Tap it to call an emergency contact directly, without navigating through your phone's contacts.

> **Tip:** Keep this updated. If your medications change or a new allergy is discovered, update the emergency info right away. This is the information that matters most when it matters most.

## 9. Account & Settings

### Changing your password
Go to **Settings → Change Password**. Enter your current password, then your new password twice. Tap Save.

### Forgot your password
On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password**. Enter your email and a reset link will be sent to you.

### Language settings
Prakrit AI is available in English and Spanish. Go to **Settings → Language** to switch.

### Logging out
Go to **Settings → Log Out**. Your data is safely stored and will be waiting when you log back in.

### Deleting your account
If you'd like to delete your account and all associated data, contact support. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

## 10. Frequently Asked Questions

**Is my health data private?**  
Yes. Your health data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. Each family member's data is accessible only to the account holder.

**Can I use Prakrit AI for multiple family members?**  
Absolutely — that's what it's designed for. You can add as many family members as you need, each with their own separate health profile.

**What file types can I upload?**  
PDFs and images (JPG, PNG) are supported. Most lab reports, prescriptions, and scan reports come in one of these formats.

**How accurate is the AI analysis?**  
Prakrit AI's document analysis is designed to be informative and helpful, but it is not a medical diagnosis. It will accurately extract and explain values from standard medical documents, but always review important findings with your doctor.

**Does Prakrit AI work offline?**  
An internet connection is required to upload documents, run AI analysis, and use the health assistant. Previously loaded data can be viewed while offline.

**Can I export my data?**  
Data export options are available from the Settings section. You can export your documents and health records for personal use or to share with a healthcare provider.

**What happens if I switch phones?**  
Your data is stored in the cloud, not on your device. Log in to your account on any new device and everything will be there.

**Is there a limit to how many documents I can upload?**  
There is no hard limit. Upload as many documents as you need — the more history you build, the more useful the insights become.

# Prakrit AI — What It Is and Why It Exists

---

## The short version

Prakrit AI is a personal health companion for you and your family. It helps you store your medical records in one place, understand what they mean, track medications, and get answers to health questions — all through a simple app, available anytime.

---

## The problem most families face

Health records are scattered. Prescriptions are on paper. Lab reports are PDFs buried in email. Medication schedules live in someone's head. When you need to recall what a doctor said six months ago, or which medicine a family member is on, or what a blood test result actually meant — you're searching through folders, photos, and WhatsApp messages.

And even when you do find the report, the numbers don't tell you much on their own. What's a normal HbA1c? Should that cholesterol reading worry you? Most people don't know, and their next doctor's appointment is weeks away.

Prakrit AI was built to fix this. One place for everything. AI that actually explains what you're looking at. A health assistant that's available at 2am when you're worried and your doctor isn't.

---

## What Prakrit AI does

### Your family's health, in one place
Create profiles for every member of your family — yourself, your spouse, parents, kids. Each person gets their own health record: documents, medications, test history, and timelines. Everything together, always accessible.

### Document analysis that actually explains things
Upload a lab report, prescription, scan, or any medical document. Prakrit AI reads it and gives you a plain-language explanation — what the values mean, which ones are outside the normal range, and what you might want to discuss with your doctor. No medical degree required.

### Medication tracking that keeps everyone on track
Add medications for any family member and Prakrit AI tracks the schedule for you. It sends reminders so doses aren't missed, and alerts you when a refill is coming up. If someone in the family manages multiple medications, this alone saves a lot of stress.

### An AI health assistant, always on
Ask anything. Follow-up questions after a consultation, worries about a symptom, help understanding a diagnosis — Prakrit AI's health assistant is available 24/7 and has context from your family's health history, so the answers are relevant to your situation, not just generic advice. Voice mode is also available if you'd rather talk than type.

### A health timeline that shows the full picture
Every document, consultation, and medication entry builds a chronological health record for each family member. Over time, you can see how things are trending — whether that blood pressure is improving, whether the HbA1c has been moving in the right direction. A health history that's actually useful.

### Health Insights across your records
Ask Prakrit AI to look across multiple reports and find patterns. It can compare test results over time, spot trends you might have missed, and prepare you for your next doctor's visit with a clear summary of what's changed.

### Emergency information, always ready
Store critical health information — blood type, allergies, emergency contacts, current medications — in Prakrit AI's emergency card. In an emergency, this is the information that matters most and is usually hardest to find. Having it ready can make a real difference.

## Your data, your control

Prakrit AI is built with privacy at its core. Your health data belongs to you — it's stored securely, never shared with third parties without your explicit consent, and protected with the same standards used in healthcare applications. Each family member's data is kept separate and accessible only to you.
